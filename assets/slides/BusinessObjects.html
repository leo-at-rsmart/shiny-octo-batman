Business Objects and Maintenance Documents

First, Business Objects

  • The most general entity in Kuali Software.
  • Everything is a business object. Well, almost everything…
  • Business Objects can be persistant. Most are persistant.

Business Objects are persistant?

Each business object has a corresponding database table.

How do we modify/maintain Business Objects? Enter Maintenance Documents

Maintenance Documents are Business Bbjects. Remember almost everything is a Business Object? Think of them as carriages, escorts, or entourages of Business Objects.

  • Maintenance Documents contain a reference to a Business Object called, DocumentHeader.
  • DocumentHeader contains the workflow metadata necessary to process a Document.
  • Business Objects are referred to via the Maintenance Document and are handled during post processing.
  • One Business Object per Maintenance Document.
